Mang Inasal

2.4★

Mang Inasal is a popular Filipino restaurant chain known for its delicious grilled chicken, which is marinated in a unique blend of spices and served with a side of rice. The restaurant's name, which means "Mr. Barbecue" in Filipino, reflects its commitment to serving flavorful and affordable grilled dishes. In addition to its famous chicken, Mang Inasal also offers a variety of traditional Filipino dishes, such as pork barbecue, halo-halo, and various seafood options. With its casual atmosphere and mouthwatering food, Mang Inasal is a favorite dining destination for many Filipinos and visitors alike.
